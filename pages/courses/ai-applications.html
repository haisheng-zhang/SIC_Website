Course Overview

Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries worldwide, from healthcare to finance, manufacturing to retail. This course is designed to help learners understand the latest developments and practical applications of AI technology, and learn how to integrate AI into business processes to improve efficiency and innovation.

Whether you are a technical professional or a business leader, this course will provide you with the essential knowledge and skills to succeed in an AI-driven future. The curriculum covers machine learning basics, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and includes real-world case studies and projects to help you apply theory to practice.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the basic concepts and differences between AI, machine learning, and deep learning
  • Master the working principles and application scenarios of machine learning algorithms
  • Learn how to use Python and related libraries to implement machine learning models
  • Gain foundational knowledge of deep learning and neural networks
  • Explore applications of natural language processing and computer vision
  • Learn how to integrate AI technologies into business processes
  • Understand planning, implementation, and evaluation methods for AI projects
  • Discuss ethical considerations and future trends in AI

Course Content

Module 1: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

  • History and development of AI
  • Differences between AI, machine learning, and deep learning
  • AI application scenarios and business value
  • Planning and implementation of AI projects

Module 2: Fundamentals of Machine Learning

  • Supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning
  • Common machine learning algorithms (Decision Trees, Random Forest, SVM, etc.)
  • Feature engineering and model evaluation
  • Implementing machine learning models with Python and Scikit-learn

Module 3: Introduction to Deep Learning

  • Basic principles of neural networks
  • Deep learning frameworks (TensorFlow and PyTorch)
  • Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN)
  • Training and optimization of deep learning models

Module 4: Natural Language Processing

  • Text preprocessing and representation
  • Sentiment analysis and text classification
  • Named entity recognition and relationship extraction
  • Language models and Transformer architecture
  • Chatbots and dialogue systems

Module 5: Computer Vision

  • Image processing and feature extraction
  • Image classification and object detection
  • Face recognition and sentiment analysis
  • Video analysis and behavior recognition

Module 6: AI in Business Applications

  • Industry case studies of AI applications
  • AI-driven business process optimization
  • Designing AI products and services
  • ROI evaluation for AI projects

Module 7: AI Ethics and Future

  • Ethical considerations and responsibilities in AI
  • Bias and fairness in AI
  • Privacy and security issues in AI
  • Future trends in AI development

Who Should Attend

  • Technical professionals (software engineers, data scientists, IT managers, etc.)
  • Business leaders and managers
  • Product managers and project managers
  • Entrepreneurs and innovators
  • Students and professionals interested in AI

Note: Advanced programming skills are not required, but basic Python knowledge will help you understand and practice the course content.

FAQ

Q: What background knowledge do I need for this course?

A: Advanced programming skills are not required, but basic Python knowledge will help you understand and practice the course content. If you have no programming experience, we recommend learning some basic Python before the course starts.

Q: Is the course conducted online or in-person?

A: The course is delivered in a hybrid format. Theory classes are mainly online, while practical sessions and project mentoring are held at our training centre.

Q: What will I receive upon completion?

A: Upon completion and passing the assessment, you will receive a course certificate from Singapore Innovation Centre. You will also gain practical AI knowledge and skills for real-world application.

Q: Can I make up missed classes?

A: Yes, all classes are recorded and available on our learning platform. We also offer additional mentoring sessions to help you catch up.
