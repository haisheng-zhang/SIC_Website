About Singapore Innovation Centre

Singapore Innovation Centre (SIC) is a Singapore Approved Training Organization (ATO), committed to providing high-quality innovation training courses in food innovation, technology innovation, sustainable development innovation and other fields.

Our mission is to cultivate innovative talents, promote industry development, and create a better future through education and training.
